Frequently asked questions

Eager to know about us! We are here to address your curiosities.
Resolve the queries arising foremost in your mind regarding us and our internships.

1. What is a “School of Interns”?

Ans. School of Interns is the learning and training internship program providing a platform where you can learn and enhance your skills which will help you immensely in the future.

2. In what fields does the School of Interns currently serve?

Ans. School of Interns deliver versatile internships in the following sectors/fields: Human Resource IT & Tech Support Content Writing Graphic Design Web Development Business Management Digital Marketing Public Relations (PR) Sales & Marketing Finance and Accounting

3. How do I apply for an internship at the School of Interns?

Ans. You can apply for an internship with us through forms available on our website and other platforms like LinkedIn and social media.

4. What do I receive after completing my internship?

Ans. After successfully completing your internship, You will get a government-approved certificate with a specific letter of recommendation. Based on your performance, you will get an opportunity to work with other eligible companies or with us too. You will get hands-on experience with real and prestigious software and portals.

5. Who can join the school of interns?

Ans. The School of interns is a learning and training platform where skills and passion are valued. Students, graduates, and other individuals such as even non-graduates can join us.

6. Do we receive any practical exposure while working in SOI?

Ans. Yes, there will be practical exposure for you as you will not only learn the skills but you will implement those skills and knowledge in various projects and tasks by using advanced software.
