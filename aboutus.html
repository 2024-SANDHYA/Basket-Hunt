About Us


School of Interns is a subsidiary of the parent company BasketHunt Pvt. Ltd. It is introduced with the idea of optimism and practical approaches to learning skills through an internship experience. We embrace constructive pragmatic methods at the School of Interns to enhance the learning skills of the students. We are passionate about providing the real-time experience of the corporate world to candidates.
Our focus is on realistic experiential learning and our goal is to build a fantastic and authentic platform where the market and the work are both true. We wish to connect big thinkers and creatives who are enthusiastic about their abilities and original concepts. We want to develop and lay a solid basis for young people who find it difficult to enter the competitive world of the corporation. The School of Interns is the platform for implementation where knowledgeable professionals will collaborate to create things that will characterize our next generation. Be it present or future, we want to establish a creative space where the skills of individuals are valued and appreciated. We aim to provide quality-oriented internships.
The School of Interns is dedicated to building a bridge between beginners and the professional world. This bridge will open a gateway of vast opportunities that will improve the present condition of unemployment. The inspiration behind the idea of the School of Interns is that everyone possesses skills in a specific field and they need proper guidance to carve a path for themselves. We are that challenging platform of guidance and implementation to support your future growth in the corporate world. We constantly work on promoting first real-time and enriching experiences for our interns who work diligently to enhance their skills and prove their caliber by creating successful projects. This platform provides the freedom to our interns to experiment with their skills and unlock their potential for further growth. We are also focused on cultivating values such as teamwork, people-oriented ideas, and continuous learning to achieve global leadership. We encourage people to believe in themselves, take on challenges, find their passion, and reach their full potential.
“School of Interns believes in the unity of ideas which are implemented by our team to create the purpose of value.”

Our Journey


“We carved our own journey from the obstacles which were meant to stop us.”

The journey of BasketHunt was started by our CEO Vishal Kumar Gupta in April 2020. BasketHunt is an e-commerce website start-up situated in Sahibganj, Jharkhand. Catering to the essential daily needs of ordinary people on their doorstep during lockdown was the first step towards the betterment of the people. Then we also expanded our business into AI, cloud computing, and web services. These expansions were also successful.
We also began investing in education on April 28, 2021, when BasketHunt Academy officially launched. Education was significantly impacted by the lockdown, and Baskethunt provided students with advanced knowledge and curriculum through online home tutoring. We provide education at an affordable cost.
In addition, on October 7, 2022, we launched the BasketHunt travel agency because we noticed a significant increase in the cost of airline tickets following the lockdown. We also decided to provide people with affordable tickets to make travelling easier and more accessible.
School of Interns (SOI) is Baskethunt’s new expenditure for the year 2023 which is like a second version of BasketHunt academy. The ongoing disregard for abilities where applicants were being turned down due to a lack of educational credentials served as the impetus for the school of interns. Through the School of Interns, our CEO decided to take the initiative and give opportunities to deserving people who are driven and enthusiastic about their professions. His motive is to provide practical exposure for the skill enhancement of these candidates who have skills but did not get the opportunity due to the lack of qualifications.
SOI is an advanced learning and training platform where we aim to contribute towards the future of those youths and candidates who are willing to take the first step in the corporate world. Our main motive is to provide internships from basic to advanced levels in different fields and interests to university students and beginners. The School of Interns is a platform where qualified applicants will be rewarded and admired. We concentrate on honing the candidate's talents in addition to their education.
On this platform, we will give you the space where you can create and implement your skills under the supervision of dedicated and expert mentors. We warmheartedly welcome you to be the part of School of Interns.
